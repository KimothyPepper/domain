--- layout: default --- Motor Safe Wearble Helmet

Project

A wearble helmet for motorcycle drivers paired with an app. This app helps drivers stay safer by tracking their routes and being informed of upcoming road conditions and weather.

They can also view and share videos that were taken during their trips. Another feature allows for those riding together the option to connect with nearby riders via bluetooth.

Features

GPS

Users can set destinations, track previous routes and view road conditions.

Video

Users can view videos of all their past routes. They can also share and delete their videos.

Phone

Users can set favourites, view contacts and connect with nearby drivers via bluetooth.

Weather

Users can track current and upcoming weather conditions.
